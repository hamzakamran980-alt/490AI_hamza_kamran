Deposit Attrition Management (DAM)

Predictive Intelligence for Proactive Banking

In modern banking, deposits don’t disappear suddenly. They leave quietly. Customers disengage. Balances drift. And by the time attrition shows up in reports, the opportunity to act is already gone.

Deposit Attrition Management (DAM) is an AI-powered solution by AI Next designed to help banks predict customer attrition before it happens — and intervene with precision.

DAM continuously analyzes customer behavior to identify early warning signals, explain why customers are at risk, and surface actionable insights directly within frontline and leadership workflows. Instead of reacting to losses after the fact, banks gain the ability to act early, retain valuable deposits, and make informed, data-driven decisions.

Beyond retention, DAM unlocks growth. By identifying contextual upsell and cross-sell opportunities, it enables smarter engagement, lower acquisition costs, and measurable improvements in customer lifetime value.

DAM is not a dashboard. It is a new operating model for proactive, intelligence-led banking.

CARE (Client AI Response & Engagement)

AI-Powered Conversations at Enterprise Scale

Every banking relationship is built on conversations. Yet traditional call centers and IVR systems struggle with scale, consistency, and context. Conversations are fragmented, customer history is lost, and engagement feels transactional rather than personal.

CARE (Client AI Response & Engagement) is AI Next’s AI-powered phone banking and engagement platform, designed to transform how banks communicate with customers — across both inbound and outbound channels.

CARE delivers intelligent, multilingual AI conversations that understand customer context, resume interactions where they last ended, and learn continuously over time. By combining real-time customer data with advanced conversational AI, CARE enables banks to engage thousands of customers simultaneously without sacrificing personalization or quality.

The result is a more consistent customer experience, improved loyalty and Net Promoter Scores, and significantly reduced reliance on human-heavy call center operations.

CARE doesn’t replace human bankers. It ensures they focus on the conversations that matter most.

Formula One UMass – Leadership, Marketing & Community Building

Formula One UMass is a student-run sports and motorsport enthusiast organization at the University of Massachusetts Amherst dedicated to celebrating Formula 1 culture, competition, and strategy. The club brings together race fans, competitive gamers, and students from all majors to experience Formula 1 both on and off the track through live Grand Prix watch parties, e-racing championships, RC racing events, and large-scale campus activations.

Vice President

As Vice President, I focus on the club’s strategic direction, operations, and organizational growth, working closely with the executive board to ensure smooth execution across events and initiatives. I help coordinate large-scale programming, support cross-team alignment, and contribute to long-term planning as the club continues to scale. These efforts played a key role in sustaining momentum across the academic year and expanding Formula One UMass into one of the most active motorsport communities on campus.

Marketing Director

As Marketing Director, I led the club’s branding, outreach, and audience engagement strategy, overseeing digital promotion, event marketing, and campus visibility. I developed and executed marketing campaigns for flagship initiatives such as the E-Racing Championships, the Minutemen Grand Prix RC racing series, and Formula 1 Grand Prix watch parties. This work directly contributed to a sixfold increase in event attendance and significant growth in overall membership engagement.

Through these roles, I’ve gained hands-on experience in leadership, marketing strategy, event operations, and community building within a fast-paced, high-energy environment that mirrors the coordination, branding, and execution dynamics behind professional Formula 1 organizations.
